Extra Curricular Activities

Introduction

National Institute of Technology,Jamshedpur promotes and encourages student to participate in varius activities to make a positive impact on the environment as these are dedicated to helping students in developing their personality, perception, learning and attitude. The main aim is to complement the academic experience of our students with extra-curricular activities that promote social and personal development. The Activity Center enables students to practice important skills and promotes independence. All activities and progress are automatically tracked and graphed, allowing teachers and parents to see the student's progress. Embedded within each activity are prompts and scrores designed to motivate the students, In addition, the acitvities can be customized for different subjects and learning levels.
