Cultural & Dramatics Society




By participating in cultural activities, the students become more passionate. It have several practical implications. The club aims to promote exposure and to increase the awareness of the students towards the finer arts of the world. From dramatics to literature, from poetry to music and dance, this is the place to come and experience it all. The club also acts as a forum to improve one's communication skills, expressivity and critical thinking power. Various enjoyable events are conducted under this club like Music, Dance, Vocals, Anchoring, Dramatics and Fashion. The main event of Cultural and Dramatic Society is cultural fest, Culfest.

The club encourages and inspires the talent of singing and the art of playing instruments like harmonium, mouth organ, flute, casio, guitar, drums etc.. The students can choose between western vocal music and non-western vocal music . Western Vocal is the kind with which we are most familiar, includes many types of vocal music. Non-western vocal music places with many ethnic groups that have rich traditions of using the voice to make music. The vocalists and instrumentalists are one of the most enthusiastic sections of CDS.

DANCE IS THE CONVERSATION BETWEEN THE BODY AND THE SOUL. And in a student's life, the balance of the body with the soul is of foremost importance. It ensures that the students are not deprived of this form of ful filled exercise.
When one starts listing the forms dances this club encompasses...the list is unending. It starts with the Indian dance forms like Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi and goes on to hip-hop, contemporary, freestyle, bollywood, mass and many more. It gives immense joy to know that this list is getting longer with every new batch.
Beat Breakers is the official name of DANCE CLUB of NIT Jamshedpur. It is a part of CDS.

Fashion model is indeed a wonderful field to work in. Society of theme modelling of NIT Jamshedpur, is stunningly innovative. During club meetings, members discuss fashion trends, work on projects, and cover areas of event-planning, sponsorship, and marketing. Sustainable fashion is also on the club's list of project goals - not just eco-freindliness, but also sustainability in terms of work environments and fair labour practices. They won several theme modelling contest.
“Some people view fashion as art, business, or creative design. Some think of fashion as streetwear, clothes inspired by urban themes, or high fashion, like designer clothes. To us in the club, fashion is whatever you want to wear to express yourself.”
