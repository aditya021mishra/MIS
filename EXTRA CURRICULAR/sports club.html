SPORTS CLUB



A sport club is a place where healthy lifestyle, which is particularly important for a modern society, should begin and develop. A sports club is also of great importance in building good attitudes that help an athelete to become involved in society and learn to function in it.The students actively participated in various sports like Table tennis, cricket ,football,badminton,basketball,volleyball as well as atheletics. The Institute have indoor games facilities inside hostel compound.They represent the college in various events held countrywide and extends excellent opportunities to participate at All India Inter-NIT/University/All India Inter-University/National levels.

Our Vision :

  • To impart training to students through different physical activities to produce healthy engineers.
  • To impart quality training/coaching in different games and sports & hone the skills of the students.
  • To help the students in overall growth of personality through the medium of physical activity.
