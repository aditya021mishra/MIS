Values and Ethics Club

Values and ethics Club, NIT Jamshedpur was formed in the year 2009 under the guidance of RV Sharma. In the institute we are conducting several programs related to Human Values and Ethics to enhance the character of the students. It is hoped that because of this effort made by the Institution ensure that the students are made aware of the problems and their possible solutions through self exploration. Also we ensure that the students internalize the fact that they have to respond to situations instead of reacting. At the same time, the Instituion will facilitate the students to identify their societal responsibilities. Through the activities conducted an effort is made to rid society from the ills prevalent. Further through these programmes we ascertain that the students realize that they have a lot of potential which when realized will propel the society forward in a positive direction.



Objectives of Values & Ethics


  • Intended to develop a set of beliefs, attitudes and habits that engineers should display concernig morality.
  • To understand the moral values that ought to guide the Engineering profession, Resolve the moral issues inthe profession.
  • To justify the moral judgment concerning the profession.
  • To inspire Moral and Social Values and Loyalty.
  • To create an awareness on Engineering Ethics and Human Values.
  • To appreciate the rights of other
